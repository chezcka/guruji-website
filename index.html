About Us

Continuous small treats are the secret to happiness! Guruji is an Indian sweets & street food restaurant located in the heart of Perth, serving a perfect blend of flavor, colors, seasoning, and fragrance from various regions of India!

Visit us to enjoy tasty and aromatic dishes that will take you on an exciting ride of flavors. Here at Guruji Indian, we offer a wide range of menu options — from delicious Indian sweets to hot and spicy street foods.

Today, we continue serving the best sweets in town and have built a deeper connection with our community, sharing our love for authentic Indian cuisine with the people of Perth and beyond.
